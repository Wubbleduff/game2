2D shooter strategy

Gameplay loop:
* Command troops to attack or defend objectives
  * Defend position
  * Attack position on a call
  * Capture flag, point, or "plant bomb"
  * Choose team where allies have strengths and weaknesses
* Play as a troop yourself
  * Shoot enemies and move between covered points
  * Take up different roles to make up for your team

TDM
Capture the flag
Search and destroy

Player:
* Should the player be stronger than NPCs?

Combat attributes:
* Movement speed
* Defense
* Firepower / strength
* Range
* Tools, specials, vision, intel
